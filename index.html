About Me

PhD Scholar in Hydraulics

I am a PhD research scholar in Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Gandhinagar, specializing in Hydraulics under the guidance of Prof. Pranab Kumar Mohapatra. My research focuses on the complex fluid dynamics of vortex drop shafts, which I investigate using experimental methods and numerical modeling.

I am passionate about developing innovative solutions for urban water management challenges. My work combines rigorous academic research with practical applications in water infrastructure design.

Beyond my doctoral research, I actively contribute to significant water management projects. From September 2022 to July 2024, I worked on the Water4Change project, an Indo-Dutch initiative. Currently, I am involved in the cNarmada initiative for integrated river basin management.
