About Me

PhD Scholar in Hydraulics at IIT Gandhinagar

I am a PhD research scholar in Civil Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Gandhinagar, specializing in Hydraulics under the guidance of Prof. Pranab Kumar Mohapatra. My research focuses on the complex fluid dynamics of vortex drop shafts, which I investigate using a combination of experimental methods and numerical modeling.

My doctoral studies are complemented by hands-on involvement in significant water management projects. From September 2022 to July 2024, I contributed to the Water4Change project, an Indo-Dutch initiative focused on creating solutions for urban water challenges in India. Since July 2024, I have been actively working on the cNarmada initiative, which aims to develop an integrated river basin management plan for the restoration and conservation of the Narmada River.
