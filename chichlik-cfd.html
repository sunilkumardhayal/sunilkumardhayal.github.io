Consultancy Project, 2025 - 2026

Chichlik Pumped Storage Project CFD Analysis

CFD-based hydraulic assessment of reservoir intake operating conditions, focused on velocity fields, free-surface behavior, pressure distribution, Froude number, streamline patterns, and anti-vortex beam performance.

Guided by Prof. Pranab Kumar Mohapatra.

My Role

Performed the end-to-end Computational Fluid Dynamics analysis in FLOW-3D HYDRO, including CFD-ready geometry preparation, operating-case setup, simulation analysis, post-processing of hydraulic results, and technical reporting for upper and lower reservoir assessments.

Methods

Compared generation and pumping cases using velocity contours, streamlines, cross-sectional plots, free-surface elevation, pressure fields, and Froude number outputs.

Tools

FLOW-3D HYDRO, Civil 3D, CAD geometry preparation, ParaView post-processing, and technical documentation workflows.

Public-Safe Technical Highlights

Reservoir Intake Hydraulics

Evaluated how flow enters and distributes near reservoir intake zones under selected operating combinations.

Anti-Vortex Beam Review

Compared flow behavior with design interventions intended to reduce adverse vortex formation near intakes.

Case-Based Reporting

Organized results across multiple generation and pumping scenarios for engineering interpretation.

Visualization

Produced technical visuals for velocity, pressure, free surface, Froude number, and streamline-based assessment.
